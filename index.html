What should I focus on this week?
Revenue is running 17% below your $12,000 target — a $2,048/week gap. Your average check of $13.70 is $1.30 under goal, so lifting it just that much across ~727 items/week recovers roughly $945/week without a single new customer.
Should I add more beef items to the menu?
You already have strong beef performers — Wagyu Shortribs ($6,186) and Shortrib Don ($4,392) both rank in your top 10. Optimize those first with a 5–8% price test before expanding. If you do add one, a mid-price wagyu option (~$18–22) would bridge the gap.
