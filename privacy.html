Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 28, 2026

HRZN AI LLC ("HRZN," "we," "us," or "our") operates the HRZN platform — an AI-powered business intelligence tool for businesses of all types. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our services.

By using HRZN, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

1. Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly to us, including:

  • Account information: Name, email address, business name, and contact details when you create an account or join our waitlist.
  • Business data: Sales data, revenue figures, labor costs, and other business metrics you upload or connect through integrations (e.g., Clover POS, Gusto payroll).
  • Usage data: How you interact with the HRZN platform, including pages visited, features used, and reports generated.
  • Payment information: Billing details processed securely through Stripe. We do not store full credit card numbers.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide, operate, and improve the HRZN platform
  • Generate AI-powered insights and reports based on your business data
  • Send you business reports, alerts, and notifications you've configured
  • Process payments and manage your subscription
  • Respond to your questions and provide customer support
  • Comply with legal obligations

3. Your Business Data

Your business data (sales figures, labor costs, menu performance, etc.) belongs to you. We use it solely to provide you with the HRZN service. We do not:

  • Sell your business data to third parties
  • Use your specific business data to train AI models without your explicit consent
  • Share your data with competitors or other businesses

We may use anonymized, aggregated data (with no personally identifiable information) to improve our AI models and benchmark performance across the restaurant industry.

4. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored securely using industry-standard encryption. We use Vercel for hosting and implement HTTPS across all connections. Business data uploaded via CSV is stored in your browser's local storage by default and is not transmitted to our servers unless you have an active account.

No method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure. While we strive to protect your data, we cannot guarantee absolute security.

5. Third-Party Integrations

When you connect third-party services (Clover, Gusto, Square, etc.), you authorize HRZN to access the data those services provide. Each third-party service has its own privacy policy governing how they handle your data. We encourage you to review those policies.

6. Data Retention

We retain your account information for as long as your account is active. If you close your account, we will delete your personal data within 90 days, except where we are required to retain it by law.

Uploaded business data (CSV files) stored in browser local storage can be deleted at any time from the Settings page.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Export your data in a portable format
  • Opt out of non-essential communications

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at hello@usehrzn.ai.

8. Cookies

HRZN uses minimal cookies and browser local storage to remember your preferences (such as dark/light mode and your selected data source). We do not use advertising cookies or tracking pixels.

9. Children's Privacy

HRZN is not intended for use by individuals under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any significant changes by email or by posting a notice on the HRZN platform. Your continued use of HRZN after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
